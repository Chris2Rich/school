Sequences


There are 3 main types of sequence.


Arithmetic series in the form:

Tn = T1 + d(n-1)

where T1 is the first element, d is the common difference and obviously, n is n.


Geometric series in the form:

Tn = a bn-1

where a is the first element, b is the common ratio and obviously, n is n.
