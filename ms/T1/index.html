Sequences


In A Level Maths there are 3 types of sequence.


Arithmetic sequences in the form:

Tn = T1 + d(n-1)

where T1 is the first element, d is the common difference and obviously, n is n.


Geometric sequences in the form:

Tn = a bn-1

where a is the first element, b is the common ratio and obviously, n is n.


Reccurence relations in the form:

Tn+1 = f(Tn)

where Tn is the nth element and f represents any function.
